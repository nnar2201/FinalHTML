Being Emotionally Torn

So what does it mean to be emotionally torn?

Being torn emotionally can simply just mean being in the state of a difficult situation.

Here are some sayings about being emotionally torn

  • “Don’t cry when the sun is gone, because the tears won’t let you see the stars.“
  • “A broken heart is just the growing pains necessary so that you can love more completely when the real thing comes along.“
  • “Let’s hope that for every time that we get hurt or break our heart, there is something somewhere being written \ to make up for it, to make the happiness due even bigger and laughters even merrier, for after all good things must happen to good people.“
  • “Sometimes, the only soul that can mend a broken heart is the one that broke it. For they are the ones holding all the pieces.“
  • “Grief is the price for love. But hearts are made to mend. Christ can do wonders with a broken heart if given all the pieces.“
  • “No matter how bad your heart is broken, the world doesn’t stop for your grief.“
  • “When we are in love, we are convinced nobody else will do. But as time goes, others do do, and often do do, much much better.”
  • “Love hurts when it changes us.“
